Overview

First TestStation combines the most common instruments on the market (Mutimeter, Oscilloscope, Power Supply and Signal Generator) with a custom-designed interconnect box full of electronics resources to offer a solid platform for anyone tasked with developing test projects to validate new products.
The connection to the UUT can be made via a custom interface board or mating cables on the TestStation to add flexibility.
Selftest boards will be used to demonstrate test sequence and validate the entire test station.
